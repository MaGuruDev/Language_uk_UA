## Privacy Policy
### Ukrainian Language Pack for Adobe Commerce / Magento 2
Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MaGuruDev ("I", "me") handles information in connection with the Ukrainian Language Pack extension published on Adobe Commerce Marketplace.

### No Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. It is a standalone translation package that only modifies the text displayed in the Magento storefront and admin panel. No connection to external servers or third-party services is made.

### No Account Required
No user account or registration is required to install or use this extension.

### No Cookies
This extension does not set or use any cookies.
